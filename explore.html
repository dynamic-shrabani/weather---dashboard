🌤️ Weather Tips

☀️ Sunny Weather

Wear sunglasses, stay hydrated and avoid long exposure to direct sunlight.

🌧️ Rainy Weather

Carry an umbrella and drive carefully on wet roads.

🌫️ Foggy Weather

Use low-beam headlights and maintain safe driving distance.

⛈️ Thunderstorm

Stay indoors and avoid open areas during lightning activity.
